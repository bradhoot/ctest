Spanish C-Test Instructions

Accent marks

You can add an accented letter or an “ñ” by clicking on the letter to insert in the Acentos panel to the left.

Try it out! Complete the word “rocío” in the blank below, adding an “i” with an accent mark by clicking on the Acentos panel.

ro

How long is the test?

This is not a timed test. There will be a total of 5 exercises, each taking about 3 to 5 minutes to complete, but there is no time limit.

What if I don't know the answer?

If you do not know an answer, you can make a guess or leave the answer blank by typing 0. To make sure you don't accidentally skip something, the form won't accept items that are left blank, so if you don't want to guess, just type in 0.
